If something's not working

The app looks empty / my logged jobs are gone
This almost always means you opened a different copy of the page than usual (a new link, or "Add to Home Screen" a second time). Your data lives on this specific installed icon, not the internet — if you tap a different link, it looks empty because it's a different copy.

Fix: go back to the same home screen icon you've always used. Don't delete it and reinstall unless David tells you to.
Copy Text button doesn't seem to do anything
Some phones block copying until you allow it once. Look for a small pop-up asking to allow pasting — tap Allow. If nothing happens at all, use the Download CSV button instead — it always works, saves a file, and does the same job.
The building I need isn't in the list
Tap "+ Other / new building" and type it in. It's completely fine — David just has to manually match it to Jobber later instead of it happening automatically. Don't skip logging the job over this.
I did more than one thing at the same building
Fill in the first task, tap "+ Add Another Task" instead of Save, then fill in the next one. Tap Save Visit only once, after everything for that stop is entered — it'll all go on one invoice, same as you'd do it yourself.
I made a mistake on something I already saved
Go to the "Logged" tab, find the entry, tap Delete, then re-enter it correctly. There's no edit button on purpose — deleting and redoing is faster and can't leave old wrong info behind by accident.
Something's broken and none of this helped
Text David. Tell him exactly what you tapped and what happened (or didn't happen) — a screenshot helps a lot if you can grab one. He doesn't need you to explain it perfectly, just what you were doing right before it went wrong.
